ELECEED

Jiyoung Yoo (유지영 Yu Ji-Yeong) is a supporting character in the manhwa, Eleceed. She is the older sister of Jisuk Yoo and the chairwoman of Shinhwa Association, an organization made up of Awakened that reside over the territory where Jiwoo lives. Her main ability is Aerokinesis, and she is one of the few Awakeners who can use Spacial Isolation. She is the current Rank #1 in Korea among the Awakened.She is currently an awakener of the world's top 50

Background

After the death of her and Jisuk's parents, Jiyoung was forced to take over the Shinhwa Association at a young age, leaving Jisuk to be in a very difficult position, as he was constantly pressured by other awakeners to fight his sister and take over.As the leader of Shinhwa, Jiyoung Yoo's responsibility is to guide new awakened ability users with the basics and providing the most basic levels of support as well as keep the awakened world hidden from the normal world.

Abilities and Fighting Prowess

Like most people in Shinhwa, Jiyoung has some type of aerokinesis ability. She is seen controlling wind. She is the strongest Awakener in Korea, and is implied to be at least within Rank 50 in the entire world, which was shown when she was able to fight on par with Vator who was stated to be within the Top 50 ranked.

Appearance

Jiyoung is an attractive young woman. People often blush and stare at her when she is out in public. She has pale skin, long raven black hair, long eyelashes, and blank black eyes. Her face normally doesn't tend to show any emotion at all, although she does occasionally blush if being sincerely praised. She is rather petite, only reaching about Jiwoo's height in heels.
